LUVReceive additional payouts to ease your worries
In the event of total and permanent disability (TPD before the age of 65), you will receive an 
additional monthly payout of 1% of the sum assured for the next 12 months or until you reach 
the age of 65 or upon death (whichever is earliest) to help ease the financial burden on your 
loved ones.
If you are hospitalised, you will also receive a daily hospital cash benefit of u p to $1006 for up to 
365 days for each period of hospital stay in Singapore to help you cope financially.
