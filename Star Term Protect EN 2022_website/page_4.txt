Star Term ProtectProtection for peace of mind
Star Term Protect provides you with coverage⁴ in the event of death, terminal illness (TI) or total 
and permanent disability (TPD before the anniversary immediately after the insured reaches the 
age of 70) during the term of the policy.
Guaranteed renewal
You can enjoy the option of renewing your policy and extending your coverage after the policy 
expires. Renewal of your policy is guaranteed³ and you may be covered to a maximum age of 84 
(last birthday).
